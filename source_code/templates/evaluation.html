Model Evaluation

Evaluating the performance of our Intrusion Detection System (IDS) is crucial to ensure its effectiveness in detecting cyber threats in vehicular networks. Our evaluation process involves several key metrics and methodologies.

Performance Metrics:

  • Accuracy: Measures the overall correctness of the model's predictions.
  • Precision: Indicates the quality of positive predictions, showing the percentage of true positives among all positive predictions.
  • Recall (Sensitivity): Reflects the model's ability to identify all relevant positive instances.
  • F1 Score: The harmonic mean of precision and recall, providing a balanced measure of the model's performance.
